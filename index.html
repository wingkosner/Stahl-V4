Stahl’s Essential Psychopharmacology

Essential. Trusted. Indispensable.

Stahl’s Essential Psychopharmacology is a suite of products that began 20 years ago as an offshoot of Dr. Stahl’s lectures about the mechanisms of disease and drug action. Consisting of not just books, but also of an online product, CME, and journal, this is the complete resource for psychiatrists, psychologists, primary care physicians, pharmacists, and nurse practitioners. Even patients and their families will find this array of products helpful in navigating the complex world of psychotropic drugs!
